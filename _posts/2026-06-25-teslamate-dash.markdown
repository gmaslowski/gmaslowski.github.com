description: A small, self-hosted, read-only dashboard for TeslaMate. It connects to your existing TeslaMate Postgres database and renders a clean, map-first view of your driving and charging history, with drives colored by speed and charging sessions clustered by location. One static Go binary, one container, no second database, and nothing is ever written back to TeslaMate.
