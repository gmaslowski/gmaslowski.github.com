description: A read-only, map-first dashboard on top of TeslaMate. It connects to the Postgres database already available in TeslaMate and draws every drive from its GPS trace, colored by speed, with charging sessions clustered by location into markers showing session count, total energy, and the AC/DC split. Timeframe and per-car filters, headline stats, layer toggles, and 3D buildings on a globe projection. One static Go binary with the web UI embedded, one container, and nothing is ever written back.
